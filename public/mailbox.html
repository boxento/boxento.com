Why it's different

Your inbox shouldn't live in another company's cloud.

Most "AI email" apps pull your mail onto their servers to do it. Mailbox keeps the mail, and the model, on your computer.

01
Stays on your machine

Mail, settings, OAuth tokens, and cache live in a local database on your computer. Nothing routes through our servers.

02
Local AI by default

On-device AI handles everyday actions for labels, summaries, and draft replies.

03
Direct desktop installers

macOS is Developer ID signed and notarized; Windows is a direct alpha installer. Both connect straight to Gmail.
